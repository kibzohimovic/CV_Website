Education Background

This is a brief summary about my education background right from my primary education to my University education.

From 2017 up to date, I am pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology and Computing at Kyambogo University through which I will be able to obtain a degree. The course has a duraton of 3 years and therefore I expect to graduate in 2020. It has been a fantastic experience studying at Kyambogo University due to the diversity of so many students and the intensive practical approach involved in the learning process.

From 2015 to 2016, I studied my Advanced Level Education at Seeta High School Mukono where I obtained a Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education after sitting my final examinations in my Senior Six. During my Advanced Level Education, I pursued a combination in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Information Technology(PCM/IT). My education in this period was truly fruitful

From 2011 to 2014, I studied my Ordinary Level Education at Seeta High School Mukono whwere I obtained a Uganda Certificate of Education after sitting my final examination in my Senior Four.

I studied my primary education from Global Junior School Mukono from 2005 to 2010 where I was able to sit the Primary Leaving Examinations in my Primary Seven and I obtained a certificate for these examinations.
